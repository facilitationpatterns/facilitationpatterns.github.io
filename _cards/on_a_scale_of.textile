---
title: '"On a scale of..."'
category: language
story: |
  "On a scale of 1 to 5, how happy are you in this project?"

problem: |
  You want to track progress of a subjective thing like safety, code quality, or happiness.

answer: |
  Ask for the amount of this thing on a discrete scale.  This allows you to quantify, measure and even graph over time things that we often think of as intangible and hard to pin down.
---
This pattern is used in [temperature].
