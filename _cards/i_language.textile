story: |
  

problem: |
  The word "you" is confrontational.

answer: |
  Don't use the word "you".  As an added benefit, the word "I" forces me to put things in my own context.
---
Using "I" allows you to take personal responsibility for what you say and to qualify things with how they affect you.  
You might want to consider that talkig about I and feelings may not be as acceptable accross cultures

h2. Antipattern

"I feel _THAT_" or "I feel _LIKE_" is cheating.  People sometimes use these phrases to impose their opinion under the guise that they are talking about their feelings.  It actually sidesteps responsibility instead of embraces.
