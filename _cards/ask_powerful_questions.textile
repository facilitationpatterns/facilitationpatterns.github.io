h2. Some Powerful Questions:

* What are you going to do about that?
* Help me understand

h2. Variations

* Ask What/How questions - these questions specifically tend to be very powerful, but not as abrasive as why
* 5 Why's - with permission, asking why 5(ish) times cuts deep into the very root of what's going on
