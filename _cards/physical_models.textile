title: Physical Models
category: discussing
story: |
  
problem: |
  When you and I are in a passionate discussion, it's often hard to see what we're both saying, and it's easy to take critiques about my position personally.

answer: |
  Use a physical model, a whiteboard drawing, something on paper, anything so that we are once again side-by-side, discussing a model that is outside of ourselves.
