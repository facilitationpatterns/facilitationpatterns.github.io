---
title: No Talking
category: generating
story: |
  

problem: |
  Contributing to a group discussion can be intimidating and difficult for anyone and is usually a struggle for introverts.  Yet the best way to find good ideas is to start with many ideas.

answer: |
  Ask the group to stop talking and brainstorm ideas quietly for a [time_box]ed amount of time.  They can [use_cards] to write them down, and may use [affinity_grouping] to sort them afterward.
---

h2. Variations

* This can be the first part of [1-2-4-all].
* Ask everyone to come up with a certain number of ideas, such as 1 or 2.
