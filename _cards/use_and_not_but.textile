---
title: Use "and" not "but"
category: language
story: |
  "Your presentation was great, but it could have been a little bit louder"
  _could be_
  "Your presentation was great, and it could have been a little bit louder"

problem: |
  Every time we use "but" the information communicated before the "but" is often lost on the receiver.

answer: |
  Use "and".
---
