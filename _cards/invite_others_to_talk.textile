---
title: Invite Others To Talk
category: discussing
problem: |
  Conversations are often dominated by the few most outgoing people in a group.  It is your job as a facilitator to make sure that everyone in a group feels heard.

answer: |
  Invite others to talk.
---
* How do you feel about that?
* How is X going?  

But "how" questions can be difficult, sometimes "what" questions can be easier:

* What was one thing that really stood out for you from last week?
* What did you notice about the exercise?

h2. Don't Forget

A direct invitation to talk can feel quite terrifying for an introvert.  Be sensitive to this and look for inclusive ways to include introverts in the discussion as well.
