---
title: Get Up
category: discussing
problem: |
  When a group sits for a prolonged time, they are more likely to get distracted.  In a bigger group, especially, they may disengage.

answer: |
  Monitor the energy and engagement in the room.  When it runs low, ask people to [get_up] to do something like [affinity_grouping] or even just [take_a_break].
