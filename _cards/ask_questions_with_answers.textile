---
title: Ask Questions With Answers
category: language
story: |
  

problem: |
  When you ask leading questions or ask a question without waiting for an answer not only do trust & safety suffer, but you miss an opportunity to [gather_data].

answer: |
  Only ask questions that you actually want answers for.  Instead of leading questions, ask open ended questions.  Instead of "Did X happen?", ask "What happened?"
---
